ABOUT KAAMCONNECT

Empowering daily wage workers through technology

OUR MISSION

KaamConnect is an AI-enabled job platform specifically designed for daily wage and semi-skilled workers. We bridge the gap between hardworking individuals and employers who need their skills, while addressing the challenges of digital literacy and job accessibility in underserved communities.

Our platform uses artificial intelligence to match workers with suitable jobs based on their skills, location, and availability. We provide multilingual support and voice-based profile creation to ensure that everyone, regardless of their education level, can access employment opportunities.
